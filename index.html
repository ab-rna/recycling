About Us

Our Aim

We intend to fund and assist local projects in the community that help the people or the environment.

Our Current Partner Project

We intend to partner and sponsor the Kingston Food Bank

The Benefits

We plan to help the community with the benefits of donating money, litterpicking with families and creating art.

Get Involved

This event will take place on the 22nd of May (Sunday) from 1pm to 5pm. The first 2 hours will consist of a community litter picking at a park along the Thames - Canbury Gardens - which at , for months, litter has remained a problem. After that, we will make our way in groups to the hall. We can wash and use the litter (and any other materials you would like to bring or find along the way) to create a mural for our community.
Entry price= £2 per person (children under 5 go free)
Canvas prices= £2.50
Paint prices= £1.25
We apologize for those attending church or sporting matches.
